We are a fully equipped veterinary animal hospital in Hong Kong with the capability to provide a range of diagnostic, therapeutic, and surgical treatments. Our team offers round-the-clock care for our patients, from puppy and kitten checks, regular wellness exams, in-house laboratory tests, thorough medical work ups (x-rays and ultrasound available), to 24 hour hospitalisation and veterinary nursing service. Routine and advanced surgical procedures (desexings, dentals, orthopaedic and soft tissue surgeries) are performed in-house by our dedicated veterinarians

Most importantly, our 30-minute consultation session allows us ample time to examine your pet and address all your concerns. We believe effective communication between vets and pet owners allows us to provide you tailored, high quality care for your loved family member.
